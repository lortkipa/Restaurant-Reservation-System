Our History

Three decades
of excellence

From a single table in Paris to six of New York's most celebrated dining rooms — a story built on craft, passion, and an unwavering pursuit of the extraordinary.

1987
The First Table

Chef Élodie Marchand opens a 24-seat restaurant in Saint-Germain-des-Prés, Paris. Within six months, reservations are booked three months in advance. The philosophy is simple: extraordinary produce, classical technique, no compromise.

1994
First Michelin Star

Maison Dorée is awarded its first Michelin star — recognition that the kitchen's commitment to excellence has reached a new level. The brigade grows to 18, and the wine cellar expands to over 1,200 labels.

2001
New York Arrives

Fine Reserve crosses the Atlantic, opening Maison Dorée New York in Midtown Manhattan. The second star follows in 2003. New York embraces the French philosophy with open arms — and full tables every evening.

2008
The Collection Grows

Osteria Luce and Shiro Nami open in SoHo and Tribeca respectively — an Italian and a Japanese concept born from the same founding values: seasonal ingredients, mastery of technique, and hospitality without pretension.

2016
Three Stars

Maison Dorée New York receives its third Michelin star — one of only a handful of restaurants in the city to achieve this distinction. Chef Marchand dedicates the honour to every member of the team, past and present.

2024
Fine Reserve Today

Six restaurants across New York, a team of 180 passionate professionals, and a digital platform that puts every reservation — at every venue — at your fingertips. The mission remains unchanged: make every meal a memory.

What We Stand For

Our philosophy

✦
Seasonal Integrity

Every dish begins with the best available produce from trusted growers and suppliers. Our menus evolve with the seasons — never forcing an ingredient before its time, never holding one past its peak.

◈
Classical Mastery

We honour the craft. Every member of our kitchen brigade trains in the classical tradition before putting their own signature on a dish. Technique is the foundation upon which all creativity is built.

◯
Genuine Hospitality

A great meal is only complete when it is shared with warmth. Our front-of-house philosophy is simple: make every guest feel that this evening was made entirely for them.

◉
Responsible Sourcing

We partner with farmers, fishers and producers who share our values — prioritising sustainable, humane and environmentally conscious practices at every step of the supply chain.

◫
Continuous Learning

Complacency is the enemy of excellence. Our chefs stage in the world's finest kitchens, our sommeliers visit vineyards, and our teams undergo training every quarter. We never stop improving.

◷
Every Detail Matters

From the temperature of the dining room to the weight of the cutlery — we believe that great dining is the sum of a thousand small decisions, each made with intentionality and care.
